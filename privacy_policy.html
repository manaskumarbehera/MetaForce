Privacy Policy for MetaForce

Last updated: September 3, 2023

This Privacy Policy describes how the MetaForce Chrome extension ("we", "us" or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The extension accesses your Salesforce session ID ("sid" cookie) solely to authenticate and fetch metadata based on your account permissions.

How We Use Your Information

Your Salesforce session ID is used for:

  • Authenticating with Salesforce services
  • Fetching metadata related to Salesforce objects you have access to

Data Storage

The extension does not store any data. All data fetched is stored temporarily in the browser's memory.

Third-Party Services

We do not share your information with third parties.

Security

We take precautions to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
